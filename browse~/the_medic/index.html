The Medic

by /u/ChristianRose--99384

"A trained medical professional of the Breach who has decided to enter the gungeon himself to kill his past."

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

His childhood home was infected with a plague which he could not cure at the time. Now, he can, and he wishes to go back into the past, cure the plague, and save his family, friends, and home.

Special attributes

He starts with four hearts, one key, two blanks.

Items

WEAPON

Morphine

Semi-auto with a Damage of 5, will poison enemies. DPS of ALL POISON ITEMS goes up by .5 with every two full hearts you pick up while holding Morphine.

PASSIVE

Oxygen Tank

Increases accuracy and dodge speed slightly. Causes double damage from fire.

ACTIVE

Breath of Death

It's a grenade that was one Bullet Kin. It switched sides, now working for the medic. It heals half a heart every three uses. After using, it will run at enemies, doing contact damage (kind of like the effect of Armor of Thorns) until death, upon which, it will explode and cause a large cloud of goop gas. Holding this item permits posion immunity.
