Personality and backstory:

The Dolly is a happy and energetic girl,she's very kind, playful, and quirky. She is brave and adventurous as well, despite her seemingly young age.

Dolly was born into a strict, religious family. Both of her parents were always working, and never really home. Her father was a teacher, and her mother was a cook at a local prison. She was given the dolly by her mother who hand made it. She was lured to the Gungeon when her doll seemingly came to life and slowly left the house.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

The past she is trying to kill is the past of of her strict lifestyle and her awfully religious and controlling . Though she was mostly lured to the Gungeon due to her doll.
