The Martian

by /u/access547

The Martian (or Popper) is a short, green alien who escaped his hidden planet of Xor.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

It was Popper himself that led the Hegemony of Man to his peaceful and quiet planet, after being tricked into giving them the planets coordinates by a Hegemony spy. Not wanting to relive the moment in his mind ever again, he flees to the gungeon to stop the spy from ever reaching his home planet

Special attributes

The Martian cannot pick up any active items, doing so will convert them into an amount of robot parts which corresponds to the tier of item he picked up (D will give 1, C will give 2, B will give three, A will give 5 and S will give 10). He also begins with one armour in addition to the normal three heart containers, similar to The Marine.

The Drone Caller

The Drone caller, The Martian's first active item. Use it to call down his drone, which (at first) shoots a bullet identical to the Zipper, however with no reload time. This item can be used as much as you like, but will only spawn one drone (at first). The drone is an AI similar to beadies spawned with the Eye of the Beholster

ACTIVE

The Drone

This is the drone which will be called down with the drone caller, enemies shot with by the drone have a 50% chance to drop at least 1 "robot part"

ACTIVE

The Martian Wrench

This is the Martian Wrench, The Martian's second active item (The Martian character passvely is able to hold 2 active items). This item is used to upgrade The Drone. "Robot parts" will be used to upgrade the Drone with the wrench, and not having enough parts will make the wrench do nothing upon use. Upgrades start at 5 "robot parts", and will cost +5 "robot parts" every upgrade. Every 5 upgrade levels will allow you to spawn an additional drone with every use of the Drone caller.

Upgrades each level are:

  • Damage: +1
  • Fire Rate: +0.3
  • Shot speed: +1
  • Force: +0.7
  • Spread: -0.5

Every 5 levels allow another drone to spawn!
