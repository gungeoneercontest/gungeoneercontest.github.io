The Hybrid

by Tidehowl#6655

An Hegemony experiment in weaponized cyborg technology, who broke out and escaped into the Gungeon to rectify the mistake that made who she is now. Sworn to protect, but disagrees with the Hegemony's definition of order.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

The day the Hybrid became a cyborg. The Hybrid was once still mostly human, but was going to be fully converted whether she wanted it or not. She broke into the deepest part of the experimental facility to combat her prisoners: A collective of brains in water tanks making the reference for this past the Sibyl System from Psycho Pass

The past she wants to change was that when she last came down here, the brains talked her down from fighting enough to distract her from a paralyzing shot. This time she won't be distracted. This time she'll be free. A rouge soldier killed his soldiers in his encampment when they were sleeping and he survived a shot to the stomach. Now he wishes to know who did it and kill him before he does harm.

Special attributes

Starts with two heart containers and two armor pieces. The slightly lower health and the mixed ratio is attributed to the delicate balance of organic and mechanical parts in her body.

Subjugator

This starting gun has long range, accuracy, and okay damage. However, is slow in the speed of its bullets and the firing rate while having a small clip. It can be expected to have just a bit lower than average DPS for a starting gun, but has a capacity for precise shots that rivals the Marine Sidearm. When Electric Eye is active, the gun's maw opens and flares red. This is a cosmetic change and the main tagline of the gun changes from "Subduing in Progress" to "Elimination in Progress"
