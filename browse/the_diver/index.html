The Diver

by /u/apuddingsatellite (AKA orbitorca#1956)

The Diver was once a sailor travelling the Gungeon's surrounding waters. Having to wear a diving suit to seek treasure in the waters, one expedition was interrupted by an eight-arm beast with just as many guns. Left for dead with a leg, a vessel, and crew taken away from them, The Diver drifts onto the shores of the Gungeon with numerous mutations.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

Suddenly attacked by Moby-Flick, or The Glocktopus, the Diver and their crew had no info on how to deal with this new monstrosity, and failed to fight it off. Left for dead, the Diver drifted to shore with mutations and a diving dress. The past they seek to kill is the reality that Moby-Flick eliminated the Diver's crew.

Special attributes

3 Heart Containers, 1 Armour, 2 Blanks, 1 Key.

Items

WEAPON

Shock Harpoon

Charged Weapon.

When the fire button is held down and released, the Shock Harpoon shoots a short-range linear AOE rectangle, similar to a smaller Air Strike or Napalm Strike.

ACTIVE

Bubble Hand

An Active Item that fires a large, short-range bubble projectile. After some time, the bubble glows.

If the glowing bubble is hit by a player projectile, it explodes, dealing AOE damage. If the glowing bubble is hit by an enemy, the bubble pops and sends a quick projectile back at the enemy, similar to Gunzookie/Gunzockie.

Has a 30 second cooldown.

PASSIVE

Diving Dress

While the Diver has more hearts, movement speed is decreased, dodge roll vulnerability increased, and damage slightly increased. Scales according to how much heart containers the Diver has.

Bonuses/Nerfs begin at receiving a fourth heart container, and have maximum effect at a sixth heart container.
