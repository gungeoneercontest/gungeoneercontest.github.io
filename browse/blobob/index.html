Blobob

by Enoshade#3207

A runaway blobulon, come to the Gungeon to change their past.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

When Blobob was still a tiny blobulin, a Blobulonian Army officer arrived to take him into the ranks of the Blobulonian Army, as is customary among them. At the time, little Blobob had a teddy bear, let's call him "Blo," who he loved with all his heart. As the officer dragged him away from his home, Blobob's teddy bear fell out into a ditch, never to be seen again. After running away from the Blobulonian Army, because Blobob was the only blobulon that did not like fighting, he ended up stumbling across the Gungeon, where Blobob swore that he would not give up until he found Blo.

Special attributes

Normal blobulons do not attack Blobob, due to him being of the same kind as them. However, blobulon officers will have a small chance to appear in any room, and they attack Blobob along with multiple other blobulons, shouting "traitor! traitor!" Bloodbulons also attack Blobob, as they are so consumed by their rage that they pay no heed as to what they are attacking.

An interesting property that Blobob possesses is that he gains a 2% attack bonus from every new gun that he picks up and absorbs into his gooey body, but also suffers a 2% walking speed reduction for each additional gun.

Blobob can pick up armour, but after absorbing it it turns into half a heart.
