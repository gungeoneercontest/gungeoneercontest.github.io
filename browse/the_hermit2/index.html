The Hermit

by /u/Rfbaseball23

The lonesome hermit escaped to the woods after his life was shattered by an evil rival.

Cane Gun - Seemingly ordinary cane converted to a gun, the hermits prized possession. Magazine 3 damage 8

Bird Call - summons a bird by mimicking the call of their fearsom leader, the bird brings treasures (key, blank, or heart)

Swift Robe - this worn out robe was passed on the the hermit from a powerful wizard. Over years its magic has weakend, it grants a small speed boost by making the wearer's body lighter

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

The hermit had a seemingly perfect life nice house in subberbs, with a wife and pet ferrit. Enter Gordon, his next door nebour. His life long rival who finally had enough of seeing the hermit happy, decided it was time to do something. He had rigged guns to shoot the minute the door to his home opened. The hermit returning from a long day's work, saw his wife and pet murdered. Unfortunately society let him down and Gorden walked away free. The hermit fled to the wilderness to live a life of solitude. Some lost adventurer found him and informed him of the gungeon to witch he went.

Special attributes

Upon completion of the past you unlock the pet ferrit. The ferrit loves chests and will lead you too the nearest one (upon room completion it will run to the door indicating the direction)
