The Cyborg

by 1cY#5100

“Bring in the test subject!” “Do you think he’ll… be able to withstand it?” “I have a good feeling about him. Soon, Cataclysm will be born…”

The Cyborg was created by a corporation he doesn’t remember in a place he does not know. All he knows is that whoever they were, they were responsible for the large laser cannon mounted to his arm. The weapon consumed his energy and his emotions; his memories seemed to become fainter with each passing moment. Every day, he was sent to a different remote village by an unmanned spacecraft and given a single voice command: to eradicate all. He had never questioned the suffering he caused until one fateful day: as he closed onto his final target, the native pleaded for mercy, offering to give the location of a mythical dungeon in which he could change his past. The helpless look on the victim’s face triggered a long lost memory of years ago, when he was just a child. The Cyborg wants to stop the deaths he caused by preventing the weapon from ever being installed upon him. By doing so, he can also discover who he once was and lead a new life.

Special attributes

Due to The Cyborg's weapon being physically attached to his arm, he cannot remove it, thus being unable to use other weapons (although he may still pick them up).
