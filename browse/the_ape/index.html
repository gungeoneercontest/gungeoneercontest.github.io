The Ape

by /u/Scottiopapyia

He is an old ape with white hair. The Ape is a seasoned melee fighter and is a little bulkier than other characters.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

The ape is trying to kill their father's Italian captor, a task he failed when he was a child.

Special attributes

The Ape starts with an extra heart and has a larger hitbox than the other characters. When holding the barrel or banana gun he receives a small damage boost. He also has a distrust for mustachioed characters and growls at them (no effect on gamepplay).
