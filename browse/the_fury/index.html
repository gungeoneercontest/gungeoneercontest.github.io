The Fury

by /u/TheOneTrueItself

The Fury is a dispatched clone of his mother ship where he was created, along with multiple other copies of himself, though this one in particular has learned to have thoughts for himself. The Fury's mission was to initially destroy life on any planet with a living species, regardless of intelligence. His last mission was a failure however.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

The Fury is attempting to kill his past, by going back, and stopping the destruction of planets, by defeating his fellow clones, and the autonomous commander that is sending them out to do so. Before this, the Fury failed at doing so, causing the destruction of the planet he was trying to save, therefore leading him to the Gungeon, in order to kill his past, and save life on the planet that is now in ruin.

Special attributes

The Fury's passive gives him an automatic +2 Curse, seeing as it is a melee weapon.
