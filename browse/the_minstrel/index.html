A talented musician who plays the lute and sings. Weapon- Lute (a trusty lute that fires small music notes but has limited ammo) Active Item- The Sound of Music (temporarily stuns enemies with his beautiful singing) Passive- Accelerando- The Minstrel slowly gets increased fire rate as he kills more enemies

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

The Minstrel used to be a famous musician back home, but when he broke a string mid-song, a One Man band stole his reputation. Now, The Minstrel hopes to reverse his actions and kill the One Man band who stole everything from him...

Items

WEAPON

The Lute

A basic lute that shoots small music notes out of its head, does 7 damage, but has a limited ammo max of 350.

ACTIVE

The Sound of Music

Temporarily stuns all enemies in the room for several seconds, giving the player a chance to recover from a recent hit.

PASSIVE

Accelerando
