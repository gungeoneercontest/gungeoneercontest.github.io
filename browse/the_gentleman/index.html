The Gentleman

by /u/WaxSkink

The son of the gunsling King. He is an entitled snob that is trained in the ways of Gungeoneering from his youth spent with his father. He is willing to take out as many bullet kin as needed to get his riches back.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

In his younger days he challenged his father to a duel for the family fortune. In his recklessness he lost and shamed his family name and his father the gunsling king banished him from the family.

Special attributes

Special attribute: Finds 25% more money. Dodges a bit slower as to not ruin his posh clothes.
