The Kernel

by /u/lamboni99 (AKA lamboni)

In the past, the Kernel was seen a a well respected, high ranking officer. He is trusted to lead thousands of men into battle and return victorious. Valiant, unflinching, and most importantly, passionate about his regiment. He has learnt the names of each and every one of them which helps keep moral high and builds mutual respect between them.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

It wasn't until his home planet of nrocpop was invaded by the Imperial Hegemony of Man that things started to take a turn for the worse.

Crops burned, citizens kidnapped for crazy scientfic experiments, buildings and structures detroyed and razed; the Kernel wanted to fight, but was afraid of what would happen to his regiment if he ordered a counterattack.

Alas, feeling defeated, the Kernel fleed his home planet of nrocpop with the remainder of his troops. Years passed and it wasn't until the Kernel stumbled upon the gungeon, with what was left of his regiment, that he realised he had made a mistake.

The Kernel had heard tales of this place and he would do anything to change what had happened in the past...
