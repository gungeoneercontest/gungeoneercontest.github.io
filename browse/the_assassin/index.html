An assassin who works for clan devoted to killing high targets, he wears a hood, a robe and a mask to cover his face. He holds a sniper rifle and a revolver, he is quick and nimble, and is quick to take the shot. His looks are unknown.

To escape from the Hegemony forces chasing him. Their past is to kill their boss who betrays him and sends him into captivity.
