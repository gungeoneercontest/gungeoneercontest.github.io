The Father

by /u/bip_bip_cabbage

In a world filled with heroes and adventurers seeking fame or redemption, The Father is... just a father. A father who lost his only son.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

After the death of his only son, The Father seeks the Gun That Can Kill The Past in order to go back when his son was alive. Only through the Gungeon can he discover why his beloved son passed away and prevent it from happening again.

Special attributes

Starts with three total hearts; however, only two of them are completely filled. Also starts with only one blank.
