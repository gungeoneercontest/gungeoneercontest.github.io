The Special Agent

by /u/albinodinoraptor

The Special Agent is a government detective. While a capable gun fighter, he is best known for his unique investigatory techniques, which have resulted in him being assigned to more supernatural cases.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

While investigating a series of grisly murders, The Special Agent tracked the killer to a mysterious portal. He attempted to apprehend the killer, but failed due to his imperfect courage. Twenty-five years later, the Special Agent enters the Gungeon determined to confront his failure, put an end to the killer, and rewrite the past for the victims.
