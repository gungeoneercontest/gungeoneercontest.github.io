The Sheriff was a respected figure in his quiet old town, but after the death of his friend, he want on a search for his murderer. The Sheriff has a tough, brittle personality and has a steady aim.

Why are they in the Gungeon? What's the past they are trying to kill?

After finding the murderer of his lost friend, the Sheriff cornered him at the edge of a river. However, he walked into a trap and was ambushed by a group of cronies and the murderer and his eye was shot out. Seeking revenge, the sheriff is now prepared for the ambush and armed with the Gungeon's deadliest weapons. (Reference to Red Dead Redemption)

Special attributes

The sheriff's eye allows him to find secret rooms and detect mimics on the floor, but he still must use a blank to open them.

WEAPON

The Whip

He primary weapon is the whip, which work as a charge up long range weapon. Charging it up fully will do 50 damage, or individual whips will do 15 damage. The range of the whip is about 3/4 the length of an average sized room. The whip has a 25% chance to stun enemies, except for bosses. When the whip is "fired" or released, it almost instantly goes to where the mouse is before drawing back. It takes 1.5 seconds to fully charge the whip.
